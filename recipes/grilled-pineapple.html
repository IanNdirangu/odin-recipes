Grilled Pineapple

Description

Grilled pineapple is so easy and really good. Hot sauce gives it an extra kick and cuts the sweetness. Serve these as snacks while the rest of the grilled feast comes together but be warned, they go quickly!

This grilled pineapple recipe is fruity, sweet, and slightly spicy. It works well as an appetizer, side dish, or unique dessert!

How to Grill Pineapple

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

These are the five simple ingredients you’ll need to make this grilled pineapple recipe:

  • 1 fresh pineapple - peeled, cored and cut into 1-inch rings
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • ¼ teaspoon honey
  • 1 dash hot pepper sauce
  • salt to taste

How to Make Grilled Pineapple

Here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you grill pineapple at home: Marinate the pineapple rings in the remaining ingredients, then grill the rings on each side for two to three minutes.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place pineapple rings in a large resealable plastic bag. Add butter, honey, hot pepper sauce, and salt.
  3. Seal the bag and shake to coat evenly. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate. Grill pineapple rings until heated through and grill marks appear, 2 to 3 minutes per side.

Recipe Tip: You can cut the pineapple slices into wedges for easier handling, then thread them onto skewers before grilling.
